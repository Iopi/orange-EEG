PlotWelchPSD
============
Plot the power spectral density across channels.


**Inputs**
- (psds, freqs)
    - psds - The power spectral densities. 
    - freqs - The frequencies.

**Use**

![](images/plotwelch1.png)

Press the _Show plot_ button to display the graph.
